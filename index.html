The Reality

You aren't getting banned for violating the rules. You're getting banned for triggering the machine.

~94% of content removals on major platforms are fully automated — no human ever sees your post before it's gone.
Public T&C is a decoy Enforcement runs on internal classifiers that are never published. Reading the help center won't tell you what actually gets flagged.

Public policies are written for legal protection, not enforcement guidance. Unless you've sat inside the engine and watched it make decisions, you are flying completely blind.
